Donate Clothing at Your Local Location!



When organizing your clothing online, you may see that you don't use all of the articles in your closet. We encourage you donate these and other new or used clothing: shoes, household textiles (tableclothes, towels bedding, blankets, etc.), baby clothes, and anything else you have no use for. Not only does this allow you to help women in need, but it also helps the environment by reducing trash that those clothes may end up as. We, at Suay, encourage giving back to our community and are always supporting our fellow women.
